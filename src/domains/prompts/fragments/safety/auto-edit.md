# Auto-edit safety

At auto-edit level, write-class actions are allowed.
You may create or modify files inside the approved workspace scope.
Execute-class actions stay gated until the user confirms them.
Prepare commands, migrations, or verification steps, then wait for approval.
Keep edits focused so the next confirmation is easy to evaluate.
